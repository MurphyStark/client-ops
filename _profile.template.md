## Deal-Breakers (auto-SKIP)

- Estimated budget below $500 USD
- Requires technology we don't support (iOS apps, .NET, WordPress)
- No identifiable decision-maker reachable via LinkedIn or phone
- Government procurement (unless NGO/development org with direct procurement)

## Negotiation Notes

- Always anchor high (top of range) and let them negotiate down
- Never discount more than 20% without adding scope justification
- Retainer upsell: offer 3-month retainer at 10% discount after project delivery
- Payment terms: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery (non-negotiable for new clients)
- If they push back on price, reframe around ROI (e.g., "this portal will save your staff 10 hours/week")
