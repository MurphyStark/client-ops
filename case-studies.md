## MLaws — Mpofu Mazhata Chambers
**Sector:** Legal | **Location:** Harare, Zimbabwe
**Services:** Website, brand identity, SEO foundation

**Problem:** Women-led law firm with no digital presence. Partners relying on referrals only, losing prospective clients who searched online and found nothing.

**Solution:** Full website with GSAP scroll animations, Source Serif Pro + Roboto typography, brand colours extracted from existing materials (`#2C2C44`, `#C4ACA4`, `#B1976B`). Competitive research across Harare law firms. SEO-optimised copy for Zimbabwe legal search terms.

**Result:** Live, professional digital presence. Positioned as the most design-forward law firm website in Harare.

**Use when pitching:** Legal sector prospects, professional services firms, firms with no website.

## Nyatsime Housing Association
**Sector:** Housing | **Location:** Kadoma District, Zimbabwe
**Services:** Estate registration portal, GIS mapping, EcoCash payment integration

**Problem:** Housing association managing plot registrations manually. No way to track payments, plot assignments, or applicant data. Committee meetings consumed by admin.

**Solution:** Flask/cPanel portal at `app.mlaws.org/nyatsime/` with GAS backend. Paynow/EcoCash FSM-based payment flow. Leaflet.js heatmap showing plot availability. Apple-inspired UI rebrand.

**Result:** Fully automated registration and payment collection. Committee now manages by exception rather than by hand.

**Use when pitching:** Housing associations, property developers, estate agents.

## WhatsApp Chatbot System
**Sector:** SME / Fintech | **Architecture reference**
**Services:** WhatsApp chatbot, FSM conversation engine, payment integration

**Problem:** Businesses losing sales because customers can't order, book, or pay outside office hours.

**Solution:** Node.js chatbot on Meta Cloud API, FSM-based conversation flows, Supabase backend, Paynow/EcoCash payment integration. Modelled on Mukuru Zimbabwe's architecture.

**Result:** 24/7 automated customer interactions with payment collection via WhatsApp.

**Use when pitching:** Any SME, logistics, retail, or service business that uses WhatsApp for customer communication.
