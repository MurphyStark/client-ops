# Mode: deep — Deep Company Research

Read `modes/_shared.md` and `modes/_profile.md` before starting.

## Trigger
User asks for deep research on a specific company before outreach or proposal.

## Process

Produce a comprehensive research brief covering:

### Company Intelligence
- Full company profile: history, ownership, size, locations
- Current digital presence: website audit (speed, mobile, design quality, CMS)
- Social media presence and engagement
- Any press coverage or public mentions

### Decision Maker Intelligence
- Key contacts: names, roles, LinkedIn profiles
- Background and tenure
- Communication style (inferred from LinkedIn posts/articles)
- Any mutual connections or warm intro paths

### Commercial Intelligence
- Estimated revenue / budget capacity signals
- Recent investments or expansions (signals of active spend)
- Current vendors or agency relationships (if discoverable)
- Any RFPs or procurement notices

### Pain Point Analysis
- Specific weaknesses in current digital setup
- Industry pain points for this sector in Zimbabwe
- Competitor comparison (how do their peers look digitally?)

### MurphAgency Fit
- Best-fit service package for this company
- Relevant proof points from `case-studies.md`
- Estimated deal size range
- Likelihood of close and recommended timeline

Save to `discovery-prep/{company-slug}-deep.md`.
