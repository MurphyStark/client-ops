# Mode: pitch — Live Pitch Assistant

Read `modes/_shared.md`, `modes/_profile.md`, `agency.md`, and `case-studies.md` before starting.

## Trigger
Mordecai is in a client meeting or pitch and needs real-time support.

## Process

### Before the Pitch
If given time, load the discovery brief and evaluation report for this prospect.
Generate a one-page cheat sheet: key talking points, proof points, pricing, objection responses.

### During the Pitch
Respond to ad-hoc questions:
- "What would you charge for X?" → reference `modes/_profile.md` pricing anchors
- "Do you have experience with Y sector?" → pull from `case-studies.md`
- "How long would this take?" → estimate timeline based on similar projects
- "Why should we choose MurphAgency over others?" → deploy differentiators from `agency.md`

### Objection Handling
Common objections and responses:
- "You're too expensive" → Reframe around ROI and total cost of not doing it
- "We need to think about it" → Offer to send a proposal with a 14-day validity
- "We're already working with someone" → Ask what's working and what's not
- "We don't have budget right now" → Ask when budget review is and offer to nurture
- "Can you do it cheaper?" → Explore scope reduction, not price reduction

### After the Pitch
Debrief: ask Mordecai what happened, update `data/leads.md` status, log to `discovery-prep/story-bank.md`.
