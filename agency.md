# MurphAgency — Agency Profile

> Full-stack digital agency based in Harare, Zimbabwe.
> Web, automation, and WhatsApp solutions for Southern Africa.

## Summary

MurphAgency is a Harare-based digital agency founded by Mordecai, delivering production-grade web systems, automation pipelines, and WhatsApp chatbots to organisations across Zimbabwe and the SADC region. Every project is led personally by the founder — no outsourcing, no juniors on your account.

We specialise in sectors where digital infrastructure is underdeveloped but urgently needed: law firms, agricultural enterprises, churches, housing associations, and NGOs. We build systems that actually get used — not wireframes, not WordPress, not templates.

## Services

### Web & Portals
- Professional websites (HTML/CSS/JS, Flask/Python backend)
- Client portals with authentication, document management, and payments
- Estate registration portals with GIS mapping (Leaflet.js)
- PWA progressive web apps for event registration and field data

### Automation & Integration
- n8n workflow automation (internal ops, notifications, data pipelines)
- Google Apps Script (GAS) systems for spreadsheet-driven workflows
- API integrations (Paynow, EcoCash, WhatsApp, Google Sheets)
- Supabase backend (PostgreSQL, auth, storage, edge functions)

### WhatsApp Solutions
- WhatsApp chatbots (Meta Cloud API, FSM-based conversation flows)
- Automated booking, registration, payment collection via WhatsApp
- Customer support and FAQ automation

### Documents & Strategy
- Investment memoranda and business profiles (investor-grade)
- Pitch decks and proposal documents
- Technical proposals for procurement submissions
