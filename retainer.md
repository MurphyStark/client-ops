# Mode: retainer — Evaluate Service/Retainer Fit

Read `modes/_shared.md`, `modes/_profile.md`, and `agency.md` before starting.

## Trigger
User wants to evaluate whether to offer a retainer or ongoing service to an existing client or prospect.

## Process

Evaluate on 4 dimensions (1–5 each):
1. **Recurring Need** — Do they have an ongoing need that justifies a retainer?
2. **Budget Capacity** — Can they afford $150–$400/month ongoing?
3. **Relationship Quality** — Is the relationship strong enough for a retainer conversation?
4. **Strategic Value** — Does this client open doors, build reputation, or lead to referrals?

Output:
- Retainer fit score and recommendation (offer / don't offer / revisit after project)
- Suggested retainer scope and price
- How to frame the retainer conversation
- Draft retainer conversation opener
